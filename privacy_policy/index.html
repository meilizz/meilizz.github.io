Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Compass & Altitude Tool (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices. Our App is designed to be simple and privacy-focused, with no unnecessary data collection or storage.

1. Information We Collect

The only permission our App requests and information it accesses is your device’s GPS location. This access is strictly necessary to provide the core functionality of the App: displaying accurate compass directions and real-time altitude data based on your current location.

We do not collect, access, or process any other personal information, including but not limited to: device identifiers, contact information, browsing history, app usage data, or any other sensitive or non-sensitive data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The GPS location data accessed by the App is used exclusively within the App itself to calculate and display compass directions and altitude. This data is processed temporarily on your device to deliver the requested functionality and is never sent to our servers, stored on your device, or shared with any third parties.

We do not use your location data for analytics, marketing, personalization, or any other purpose beyond providing the core features of the App.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store any user data—whether location data or any other information—on our servers, in the cloud, or on your device. Once you close the App, all temporarily processed GPS data is immediately discarded and not retained in any form.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We never share, sell, rent, or disclose your location data or any other information to third parties, including advertisers, analytics providers, or other service providers. Our App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, trackers, or tools that collect user data.

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over the GPS location permission for our App. You can grant or revoke the location permission at any time through your device’s settings. Revoking the permission will prevent the App from displaying accurate compass and altitude data, as these features rely on GPS access, but will not affect your device or any other apps.

Under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR, CCPA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. However, since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete.

6. Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Since our App does not collect, store, or transmit any user data, there is no risk of data breaches, unauthorized access, or misuse of your personal information through our services.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any updates will be posted in the App, and the effective date will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: bigdatacloak@gmail.com

By using our App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to our limited data practices as described herein.
